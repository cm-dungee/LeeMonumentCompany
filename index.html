Lee Monument Company

And the Lee Monument Foundation

Welcome to Lee Monument Company! We specialize in designing and carving quality custom headstones, memorials and monuments. We offer over 10 years of family tradition, experience and compassion in helping our clients capture the memory of their loved ones. Lee Monument offers superior quality headstones that are monitored closely throughout the manufacturing process.

Lee Monument Company is proud to present the Lee Monument Foundation, which is an organization that helps families who can't afford to purchase a headstone for a child. Please call or click HERE to learn more. We also offer services such as inscribing final dates (date of death) and other inscriptions on monuments that are already set in the cemetery. We can also perform cleaning and restoration on neglected or damaged monuments. We are proud to offer excellent products at competitive prices, all backed by exceptional service. We invite you to pay a visit to our showroom, or call us if you would like for us to visit you. We look forward to hearing from you soon.

Payment plans available. No interest or late fees.
